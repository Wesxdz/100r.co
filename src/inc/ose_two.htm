Transplay is my vision for a post-GVCS intentional community

In the midst of an abundant AI summer, fruits cached by the billion, a winter's biting chill strikes me.

It is a helpless gust that lulls the sparse network, a global half-playground disassembled. Deus ex machina capitalism: the open source hardware economy unactualized.

Ephemeral intelligence condensed into the present frame: a village nestled in the heart of the simulation where humans play.

They are in a collective flow, an MMORPG collaboration nurtured diligently by open source environments, robots, and NPCs. Mediated by creative civic algorithms, participants are engaged in open source civilization systems integration in fashion-forward casual wear. Laughing pleasantly at arXiv papers as though they were Calvin and Hobbes comics!

No, not for something as drab as comfort through specialization...

The purpose is not even implicit any longer. The transhumanist crisis ethics held are too safety critical to crumple like a monotone love letter, to risk freefall in foreign markets. Yet, infinite marshmallow-maximizers could not draw forth austerity to meet the measure.

Forking civilization from the GVCS: I want more AI, robots, veganic food, transhumanism, and indie gamedev flavors.
